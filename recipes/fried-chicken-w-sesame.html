Fried Chicken with Sesame

I've actually never tried this but it sounds delicious. It's from Ann Rogers' The New Cookbook for Poor Poets.

Ingredients

  • 4 pounds of chicken, cut for frying
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 2 tsp paprika
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp pepper
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped peanuts
  • 1/4 cup sesame seeds
  • 1 stick of butter

Steps

  1. Beat the egg and mil together. Dip chiken pieces into this and then into the flour mixed with baking powder, salt, pepper, paprika, nuts and sesame seeds.
  2. Fry the chicken until it is nicely browned. Add a tbsp of water and cover pan loosely. Cook over low heat for about 45 mins or until chicken is tender.
  3. If you want gravy, add 1.5 cups of milk to drippings in pan and heat to scalding. Mix 4 tbsp flour with an extra half cup milk, add to pan and cook, stirring constantly until thick and smooth. Add salt, pepper, lots of paprika and a dash of sherry.
