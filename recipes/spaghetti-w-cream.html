Spaghetti with Cream and Bacon

Description

This is the first pasta I learned to cook, and it's very difficult to get wrong

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 1/2 cup of cream
  • 1/2 cup of milk
  • 1/2 cup of diced onion
  • 5 strips of bacon, chopped
  • 1/2 tbsp of butter
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp of salt
  • ground pepper
  • 1/4 cup of parsley, chopped

Steps

  1. Fill a large pot with water and add the pasta when it gets to a rolling boil.
  2. While the water boils, melt the butter on a saucepan. Add the onion, the garlic, and the bacon, and cook until the bacon is crispy.
  3. Add the pasta to the water pot and let it cook. Try single pieces occasionally to check if it's ready (you can throw them at a window. If they stick, they're ready).
  4. Continue stirring the bacon and add the cream and the milk. Add the salt and pepper and let the flavors integrate.
  5. Strain the pasta. Add the parsley to the sauce and give it a final stir.
  6. Pour the sauce into the pasta and serve with grated parmesan on top.
